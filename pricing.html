How Payments Work

Payment will be due at the first lesson of each month. So before the first lesson of the month, check the calendar and count how many lesson days there are, then if you have any prior engagements, pay accordingly. If you already know you’ll be gone for a lesson in the month, let me know when you send your check on the first monthly lesson. Any missed lessons beyond that will not be refunded.

In the event that someone is sick, we can attempt a make-up, but know it’s not always guaranteed. Flute is $25 per lesson, piano is $15. I specialize in beginner piano. Flute I teach beginner to advanced.
