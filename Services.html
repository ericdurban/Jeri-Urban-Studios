Services

Age group: 5 years old to adults.

Specialty: I specialize in teaching beginner piano students, while for flute, I offer lessons ranging from beginner to advanced levels.

What I'll Help You With!

Investing in piano and flute lessons is a powerful way to enrich your life and improve yourself both personally and intellectually. Learning these instruments not only enhances your musical skills but also sharpens cognitive abilities such as concentration, memory, and problem-solving.

The discipline of regular practice fosters patience, resilience, and a sense of achievement, while the ability to express emotions through music can boost your emotional well-being. Whether you're looking to develop a new hobby, boost your creativity, or deepen your self-discipline, investing in music lessons offers a lifelong path of growth and self-improvement.

Piano

  • Correct posture
  • Correct finger placement
  • Pedaling

Flute

  • Correct posture
  • Air support
  • Tonguing and the concept of resonance
  • Step-by-step embouchure formation
