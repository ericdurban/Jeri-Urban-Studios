A Little About Me...

Hello! Thank you for investing time into yours or your loved one's interest in music. I have a passion for music and performing and would love to be the one to help nurture that interest. I'm a wife, mother, and expert musician. I received a bachelor's degree in music with flute performance as an emphasis. I have played as principal flute in professional ensembles, such as orchestras, bands, quintets, etc. I have played for almost 20 years, and have been teaching for 8 years.
