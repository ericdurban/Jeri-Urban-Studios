A Little About Me...

Hello! Thank you for investing time in your or your loved one's interest in music. I have a passion for music and performing and would love to help nurture that interest. I'm a wife, mother, and expert musician. I hold a bachelor's degree in music with an emphasis in flute performance. I have played as principal flute in professional ensembles, such as orchestras, bands, and quintets. I've been playing for almost 20 years and have been teaching for 8 years.
